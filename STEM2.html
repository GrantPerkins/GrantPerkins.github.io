STEM 2

Juniors at MAMS, during C and D term, work in partnership with a charitable organization to create a product to help people with disabilities. These products typically are hardware-focused.

Music Motions

My STEM 2 project was in combination with my Apps for Good project. TO learn more about our app go here. The hardware side of our project was to create a portable battery holder to mount our battery to the tablet stand. This allows the user to use our product for an additional hour at least. Below is a picture of our app and prototypes.

We 3D printed our battery holders, and CAD'd them ourselves in SolidWorks. The current version holds our battery perfectly. At some point, we may add a lid to the top of the case, in case it goes upside-down.
